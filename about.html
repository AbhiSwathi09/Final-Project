Welcome to Disney

Disney is a multinational entertainment and media conglomerate known for its iconic films, theme parks, and characters. Founded by Walt Disney and Roy O. Disney in 1923, the company has grown into one of the largest and most beloved entertainment companies in the world.

At Disney, we strive to create magical experiences for audiences of all ages, whether it's through our timeless animated classics, thrilling theme park attractions, or innovative new projects. Our mission is to entertain, inspire, and create joy for generations to come.

Thank you for being a part of the Disney family!
